About Me

WHO ARE YOU?

Artist. This word describes me the best. Art is knowledge and art is life. As humans, we have certain limits we cannot trespass, these vary from sensory to metaphysical limits. The human is a wonderful and interesting being which should be fully explored; however, the body we have is simultaneously a home and a prison for our mind. Art is a medium we can use to express and give shape to our feelings and thoughts. I am an actor in the hope and process of achieving enough knowledge and experience to bring the best performances to the screen.

I want to be able to offer infinite alternative realities to viewers, where they can explore themselves and the universe. I know I have a long difficult road ahead, and I am ready and willing to fight through it. We have to incite change and provoke thought. If you are reading this it means it’s already started. I’m currently based in Berlin and working on some productions. I’m always excited about new work, so if you believe we could be a good team, don’t hesitate to contact me.

Mario.
